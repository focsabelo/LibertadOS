# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Libertad OS is for one primary user managing personal finances with a long-term freedom goal. The user is usually doing quick capture, monthly review, or decision support, often with incomplete thoughts written in natural language rather than clean accounting entries.

## Product Purpose

Libertad OS helps measure financial freedom, understand the impact of spending and investing decisions, and convert messy personal notes into confirmed financial data. Success means the user can capture expenses, income, investments, debts, and decisions quickly, review the detected structure, and only then let confirmed data affect the dashboard.

## Brand Personality

Calm, serious, premium. The product should feel like iCloud Notes crossed with a focused financial dashboard: familiar enough to trust, restrained enough to use every day, and motivating without sounding like a trading app.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic fintech dashboards, loud crypto styling, gamified money apps, marketing-page hero layouts, decorative gradients, cluttered accounting software, and any flow that saves financial data without explicit confirmation.

## Design Principles

1. Capture first, account later: natural-language entry should feel effortless, while structure appears as a review layer.
2. Confirmation is sacred: detected data must not become real financial data until the user explicitly confirms it.
3. Show financial consequence: key decisions should reveal their impact on the freedom number, monthly spend, or net worth.
4. Premium means quiet precision: hierarchy, spacing, and copy should reduce anxiety rather than decorate the interface.
5. Familiar objects, clear states: notes, detected items, and confirmed transactions should look distinct and behave consistently.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for contrast and keyboard navigation. Use clear focus states, readable text sizes, 44px touch targets where practical, and reduced-motion-safe transitions. Color should support meaning but not be the only way meaning is conveyed.
